What is this?

So in this app is a series of hypothetical 'workouts'.

And we want you to show us how you'd log these in your workout notebook.

...preferably using your phone's voice-to-text converter..

Everything in here is anonymous, no one knows who posted what. So don't be embarassed.

Also feel free to post many different varitaions of your results! We actually encourage that

Some tips...

Your phone only converts speech into existing words...

..it does not know what an "AMRAP" is,
so instead of saying that, try saying "as many rounds as possible".

..it might not understand when you say "2k row".

..if you say "21 15 9" too fast (as in 21-15-9 Fran),
your phone might think you said "21159".

Examples...

Workout: "5x4 deadlift @80%"

You say: "Five by four deadlifts at 245, 255, 265, 275 pounds"

Workout: "3rft 15 kb 55lbs 21 pullups"

You say: "Three rounds for time of 15 kettlebell swings at 55 pounds 21 pullups in 5 minutes 32 seconds"

Try speaking naturally and however is comfortable for you. We'll do the work of understanding it.
