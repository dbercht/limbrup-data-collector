LimbrUp

This is an application built to collect information on how athletes log their workouts :)

We want to build the next-generation method of logging your workouts...
By speaking to your phone.

So when 'logging' your workout, try and speak to the phone.
Remember that the speech-to-text might not be super intelligent.

Every input is anonymous to the system and to others.

We only track what you've done based on cookies (that stay on your computer).

Checkmarks () indicate what you've done.
